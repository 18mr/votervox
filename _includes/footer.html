Who Are We?

18MR.org is a nonprofit online hub for social change and civic participation serving Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders (AAPIs) in the United States. We serve a network of more than 80 organizational partners–civic engagement, community organizing, policy advocacy, and new media groups–plus over 80,000 individual members.
